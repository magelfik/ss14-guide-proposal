## Explaining

Please refer to the v1 for basic explanations.

### Merging the moderation team

@lottorenato pointed out Discord bots could logs users banned or kicked from the Discord. Therefore logging the admin's actions and avoiding silent bans. This is a great opportunity to merge the Discord team and IG moderation into one team : _the moderation team_.

### Reworking the Central Committee

The Central Committee is like Central Command (unfortunately, they both have the same acronym for now) : only intervening for central affairs. Those are rules, escalated disputes, and so on.

### Adding inner roles inside teams

To allow teams to run smoother in decision making and handling disputes, the new teams now have different levels of hierarchy. All report to the Central Committee.
