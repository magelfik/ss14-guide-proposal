# Why does this matter

The administration team of a Space Station 14 server is like a company (except it usually loses money).
In any company, or more generally structure, there is a need for a structure, whatever it is.

A company that doesn't have a well-thought structure gets problems earlier and that gets us nowhere.

## What does that have to do with SS14

SS14 has modules, just like a company has departments. If you give every single employee an all-access
pass, you may end up incentizing malicious actions.

For instance, an admin banning the user from the game and the Discord to prevent them from starting an
admin complaint.

There are lots of consideration for a hierarchy that works.
